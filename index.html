Jasprit Bumrah declared fit

Jasprit Bumrah has been declared fit and he will now be available for selection for India's third Test against England at Trent Bridge starting August 18. Bumrah missed all of India's matches in England so far - 3 T20Is, 3 ODIs and 2 Tests - after injuring his left thumb in the first T20I against Ireland in Dublin.
