Dilbar Dilbar

The song 'Dilbar Dilbar' from John Abraham-starrer Satyamev Jayate has been released on Wednesday. The song is a refurbished version of the iconic song from Sushmita Sen and Sanjay Kapoor's movie 'Sirf Tum'. The original Dilbar song was crooned by Alka Yagnik and Vinod Rathod. The new revamped version is sung by Neha Kakkar and Dhwani Bhanusali and also incorporates Alka Yagnik's voice. The recreated music has been given by Tanish Bagchi and the modified lyrics have been penned by Shabbir Ahmed and Ikka. The essence of the iconic 90's number does not get lost in the remix. The first song Of the movie Satyamev Jayate features actor/model Nora Fatehi who sizzles in the song and has impressed the audience with her sultry moves. Nora in a shimmery red skirt and blouse combo looks smoking.
