Akshay Kumar delivers an Independence Day hit




Akshay Kumar is all set to gift his fans his upcoming film Gold. The film, that tells the story of India's first Olympic gold win as an independent nation, is hitting the screens on August 15 this year. The film will clash with John Abraham's Satyamev Jayate on Wednesday at the box office. While both Gold and Satyamev Jayate are eyeing the Independence Day window, trade analysts suggest that the Akshay Kumar film will have an edge over John Abraham's movie. As Gold hits the theatres tomorrow, see what the early reviews have to say. Gold, directed by Reema Kagti, revolves around independent India's first Olympic gold win. This feat was achieved by the Indian men's hockey team on August 12, 1948. While the film has been inspired by true events, the makers have taken certain creative liberties.
